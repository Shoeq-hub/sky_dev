What is the health check

A health check in a company refers to a comprehensive review or assessment of a particular aspect of the organization to ensure everything is functioning properly and efficiently. The term can be used in different contexts depending on the focus.

What is the purpose of the health check

Catch problems early before they escalate.
Optimize performance and efficiency.
Ensure compliance with regulations.
Support decision-making with data.
Foster a proactive, improvement-focused culture.
